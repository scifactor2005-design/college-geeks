Robotics

Physical intelligent agents

Introduction

Robotics combines AI with mechanical and electrical engineering to create physical agents that interact with the real world.

Components

  • Sensors (vision, touch, proximity)
  • Actuators (motors, servos)
  • Control systems
  • Navigation and mapping

Applications

Manufacturing automation, autonomous vehicles, medical surgery, space exploration.
